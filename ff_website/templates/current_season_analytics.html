Strength of Schedule

This metric is calculated using a similar rotisserie fashion. The values were generated by looking at the points scored by each team's OPPONENT on a given week. If they played the team that scored the most points, they recieve 11 points, and if they played the team that scored the fewest points, they recieve 0 points. A higher number indicates a harder Strength of Schedule.
